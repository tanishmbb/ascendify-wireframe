THE PROBLEM

Four Broken Systems. One Fix.

01

The Average Fallacy

GPA discards niche talent in specific domains. Students with low GPA but high domain mastery are filtered out before the first conversation.

→

8 Independent Leaderboards

Elo + XP per domain, not blended. Everyone is Top 10% somewhere. No single axis determines worth.

02

Invisible Talent

Capable introverts with zero campus visibility. Current system favours extroverts — builders stay hidden.

→

API-Auto-Built Profile

GitHub, LeetCode, CodeChef webhooks build your profile automatically. No self-promotion required.

03

Resume Dishonesty

Unverifiable claims, identical-looking CVs. Companies waste ₹80,000+ per recruitment cycle on manual interviews.

→

Physical Panel Verification

Saturday Bootcamp + Admin Portal scoring. Timestamped audit logs. Every achievement has a source.

04

Motivation Decay

No feedback loop outside exam season. Mid-tier and bottom-tier students rely on attendance registers and semester-end grades.

→

Gamification Engine

Daily quests, streaks, duels, XP pop-ups, leaderboard movement. Constant feedback that feels like progress.
