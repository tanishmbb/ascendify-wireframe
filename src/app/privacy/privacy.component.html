Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 15, 2026

1. Data Collection

We collect information you provide during onboarding, including academic details, connected platform data (e.g., GitHub, LeetCode), and performance metrics during bootcamps and duels.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to generate your Ascendify Domain Radar, match you with relevant companies, and provide personalized quest recommendations. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Shadow Mode & Anonymity

When Shadow Mode is active, your personally identifiable information (Name, Gender, College) is hidden from recruiters. They will only see your performance metrics, domains, and badges. Your identity is revealed only when you explicitly accept a recruiter's request.

4. DPDP Compliance & Right to Deletion

In compliance with the Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your data at any time. You can initiate a full data deletion request from your Account Settings.
